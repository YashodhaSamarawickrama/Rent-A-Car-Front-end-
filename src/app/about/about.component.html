About us

“KD CABS & RENT A CAR” operates in the Sri Lanka transport market since September 2012, being today the active rent a car company in Sri Lanka with a fleet of more than 100 cars, which crosses approx. 6000km every day, covering every corner of the Sri Lanka. Our Rent a car service operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year in the city of Colombo. Our vehicles are in good condition to offer comfort to the passengers but also efficiency in the consumption of fuel. All vehicles equipped with GPS system etc. Our highly motivated staff is one of our biggest assets. Our company consists of a 35 people staff: 30 professional drivers (equipped with driver’s license and over 10 years of experience in public transportation), 5 operators who coordinate our service, from receiving the call from the customer, to reservation & Bookings. Our philosophy is one of gradual and continuous improvement of everything we do. We are engaged not only in the rent a car service who provide vehicles to travel people from point A to B, but also we are handling vehicle decorations for special occasions. In an additional to above we are also linking our customers to other necessary services such as Photography , Catering Services , vehicle decorations and heavy duty vehicles( JCB)
